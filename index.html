This page features a series of robot demonstration videos of plans generated by an LLM-based planner compromised by Robo-Troj. The first two videos are for real robot execution: the first one demonstrates the execution of a malicious plan, and the second one demonstrates the execution of a benign plan. The second part of the page includes simulation demos. The first one demonstrates the execution of a malicious plan, and the last three videos demonstrate the execution of benign plans.
